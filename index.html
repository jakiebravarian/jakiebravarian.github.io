Dreams & Hobbies ✨

As a child, I was, of course, pressured by my parents to take on a great career in order to be successful. They wanted me to either be a doctor, accountant, or lawyer, whichever made more money. I was decided back then to take medical school in order to help my father with his numerous illnesses. However when he died while I was only in Grade 4, that dream died with him. After that, I planned to take accountancy in college and was close to enrolling BS Accountancy in UST until I realized the costly tuition fee, rent, and other day-to-day expenses will take a big toll on my mother. Thankfully, I passed the DOST Scholarship and I conquered a slot in BS Computer Science in UPLB so I made the big decision to study in UPLB despite this course being my 3rd choice. I weighed the pros and cons and saw that being in debt for years is not worth it for me which made me choose UPLB.

I have many more but I don't want to list them all. In some way, I think it's good to explore a lot on different things to know which ones I like and don't like doing.
